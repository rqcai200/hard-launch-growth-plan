Why this matters for PMs

AI prototyping tools like Cursor, Lovable, and Claude Code let you build working software through natural language. For PMs, that means going from product idea to clickable prototype in an afternoon, without waiting on engineering. Companies like Shopify now test AI prototyping in their PM interview loops. As Deepak Singh, ex-Amazon PM, puts it: "For the first time, you can actually show what you can build, your product sense and everything, by creating a portfolio of products." PMs who can prototype make better product decisions, communicate more clearly with engineering, and move faster from insight to validation.

1

Foundation

Build your first prototype this week
Key skills to master
  • Build a functional prototype in under 30 minutes using v0, Lovable, Bolt, or Cursor. No coding required.
  • Pick the right tool for the job: quick UI mockups vs. full-stack apps vs. working inside an existing codebase
  • Write prompts that produce working software by starting with structured specs, not open-ended requests
  • Deploy and share a live link with your team using Vercel or GitHub, same day
