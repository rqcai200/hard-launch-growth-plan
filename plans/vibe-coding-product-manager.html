Why this matters for PMs

AI prototyping tools like Cursor, Lovable, and Claude Code let you build working software through natural language. For PMs, this means going from product idea to clickable prototype in an afternoon without waiting on engineering. Companies like Shopify have added AI prototyping to their PM interview loops. As Deepak Singh, ex-Amazon PM, puts it: "For the first time, you can actually show what you can build -- your product sense and everything -- by creating a portfolio of products." The role is changing: PMs who can build prototypes make better product decisions, communicate more clearly with engineering, and move faster from insight to validation.
